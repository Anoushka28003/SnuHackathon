Powerful Features

Our comprehensive communication tools are designed to make interaction effortless and inclusive for everyone

Gesture Recognition

Convert hand gestures into text messages in real-time. Our advanced AI understands various sign languages and hand movements to facilitate smooth communication.

Voice to Text

Speak naturally and watch your words transform into text instantly. Perfect for quick messaging and maintaining the flow of conversation.

Text to Speech

Convert written messages into natural-sounding speech. Help blind users access information through audio output with our text-to-speech engine.

Text to Gestures

Transform text messages into visual gesture animations. Display sign language representations to help deaf users understand spoken content.

Accessibility First

Built with accessibility at its core. High contrast modes, large text options, and screen reader compatibility ensure everyone can use WEHUMAN.

Conversation History

Never lose important messages. Store and retrieve your conversation history anytime, with options to export and share discussions.

About WEHUMAN

WEHUMAN was born from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves to communicate freely, regardless of their physical abilities. We believe that communication is a fundamental human right that should be accessible to all.

Our platform integrates cutting-edge technology with user-centric design to create a seamless communication experience. Whether you're deaf, mute, blind, or simply want to communicate with someone who is, WEHUMAN bridges the gap.
